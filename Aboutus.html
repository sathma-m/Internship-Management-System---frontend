About Our Pre-Internship Management Hub

The **PRO LAUNCH** system was developed to create a direct and transparent pipeline between our highly capable students and industry opportunities. Recognizing the complexity of managing applications manually, we have streamlined the entire process.

Our Core Mission

Our mission is to simplify the pre-internship journey for students and streamline the application vetting process for our program coordinators. This system ensures every student has a fair chance to apply and every application is centrally reviewed by the Coordinator/Admin team before being forwarded to organizations.

Focus: The Pre-Internship Phase

To ensure a successful and timely launch, we currently focus solely on the crucial **Pre-Internship** phaseâ€”finding, applying for, and securing your placement. This focus allows us to provide a robust and flawless experience in these key areas:

Key Features:

  • **Centralized Opportunity Board:** Access a curated list of all available internships posted by the Coordinator on behalf of partner companies.
  • **Integrated Profile Builder:** Easily create and maintain your professional profile and upload your CV, which is instantly used for one-click applications.
  • **Real-Time Application Status:** Track the current status of all your submitted applications (Applied, Under Review, Accepted) directly from your student dashboard.

System Users

The platform supports two main user roles to manage the flow efficiently:

  • **Students:** Browse listings, manage their profile, apply for positions, and track application status.
  • **Coordinators/Admins:** Post opportunities, manage listings, and review/approve student applications.
